🚗 The Story Behind Parking Tom

🚗 Priča iza Parking Tom-a

It started with a simple problem: I kept forgetting where I parked my car days ago. Sound familiar?

Then my wife and I realized we both had the same issue with our 2 cars. We don't drive every day, so occasionally we'd completely forget where we left them!

That's when I decided to create a simple app where we could both check the location of any of our cars. Of course, we'd have to remember to save the location each time - but that's still better than wandering around looking for your car!

As I built the app, I realized it could be useful for so much more than just parking. You can use it for meeting up with friends in a crowded park, saving that amazing restaurant you found, or setting a rendezvous point for your group trip.

The best part? You can create joint accounts with your partner, parents, kids, or anyone you trust. Everyone sees the same locations and can add their own!

Pro tip: You can use any email account - existing or made up. But I recommend using a real email address (who knows how many people might try "buddies@beer.com"!).

I hope Parking Tom makes your life a little bit easier. Enjoy! 🧭

Počelo je s jednostavnim problemom: stalno sam zaboravljao gdje sam parkirao auto prije nekoliko dana. Zvuči poznato?

Tada smo moja žena i ja shvatili da oboje imamo isti problem s našim 2 auta. Ne vozimo svaki dan, pa povremeno potpuno zaboravimo gdje smo ih ostavili!

Tada sam odlučio kreirati jednostavnu aplikaciju gdje oboje možemo provjeriti lokaciju bilo kojeg od naših auta. Naravno, moramo se sjetiti spremiti lokaciju svaki put - ali to je ipak bolje nego lutati okolo tražeći auto!

Dok sam razvijao aplikaciju, shvatio sam da može biti korisna za puno više od samog parkiranja. Možeš je koristiti za sastanak s prijateljima u prepunom parku, spremanje tog nevjerojatnog restorana koji si našao, ili postavljanje točke sastanka za grupni izlet.

Najbolji dio? Možeš kreirati zajedničke račune s partnerom, roditeljima, djecom ili bilo kim komu vjeruješ. Svi vide iste lokacije i mogu dodavati svoje!

Savjet: Možeš koristiti bilo koji email račun - postojeći ili izmišljeni. Ali preporučam korištenje prave email adrese (tko zna koliko ljudi bi moglo pokušati "buddies@beer.com"!).

Nadam se da će ti Parking Tom olakšati život. Uživaj! 🧭
